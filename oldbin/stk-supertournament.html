Welcome to the STK-SuperTournament!

The next STK-Supertournament is taking place between the 01th September and the 19th September 2020.

This Tournament is for everyone but especially pro players are very welcome to join.

If you want to participate just run /join on the Server "STK Supertournament.

To edit the time poll just run /ican [time] on the Server "STK Supertournament. The time has to be given in a certain format. For example mo16 means Monday 16 UTC, 17 means 17 UTC on each day, weekdays18 means 18 UTC on weekdays, weekends means on weekends on any time, we means on Wednesdays at any time, all means at any time. With the command /icant [time] you can remove times you already chose whith /ican [time]. If you have specific days on which you can't play just tell me in Discord.

The teams are created with the goal to get strong and balanced teams of three players. Each team should include a suitable defender who can take the goalkeeper position. The organizer will make suggestions for teams which can be discussed by the participants in the next seven days.

This tournament is played without substitutes. If your timetable changes and you know you cannot come to a match, please tell it 24 hours before match start. If not all participants are their, the match will be rescheduled and a yellow card will be given to missing players if not told 24h before. If the teams are not complete at the rescheduled date, the game is played with two or one players per team.

Each match includes two games. The first game is played on icy soccer field, the second on a choosen addon and the third on supertournament field. The tournament is played in two rounds, so each teams play twice against each other. The home team (defined as the team mentioned first in match) has the right to choose the addon in second game.

7. Rules

The first three games are played with 10 minute time limit. The first game is played on ice, the second on an addon track (without ice/tournament field/supertournament field) and the third match is played on supertournament field. The addon can be selected by the home team of the match (e.g. Team A vs Team B means that Team A can select the addon). Each addon can only be taken once, so in the tournament 12 different addons are played.

Yellow cards are given by the referee if instructions are not followed (however always a warning is given before) or if you are more then 5 minutes late to a game. Two yellow cards mean that the player has to play the next match whith Tux. The third yellow card means that a game has to be played with Gnu. The fourth yellow card means that a game has to be played with Pidgin. After five yellow cards the player misses one match and is not substituted.
